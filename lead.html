What To Consider First When You Start Planning A Bathroom Renovation?

Before you embark on any renovation project, the first thing to consider is what you would like to change about the space. For bathrooms, you may want to change or improve on several things such as:

  • Upgrade the flooring and fixtures

  • Add more space in the room or make it look less cluttered

  • Add an extra feature like a half-bath to your home

  • Add a walk-in shower or a tub

  • Additional storage by taking advantage of extra space such as under the sink

  • Add exciting features like mirrors, a built-in vanity

  • Make the bathroom safer and more functional such by adding a bench or handicap toilet area

  • How Much Does The Bathroom Renovation Project Really Cost?

    First, you’ll need to consider the labor cost of your bathroom renovation. Most contractors are paid on an hourly rate which varies depending on their level of experience and qualifications. You can get a contractor for as low as $45 per hour or more if they are significantly experienced. You may choose a contractor who offers all-inclusive services. This means that you’ll have access to designers, plumbers, electricians, and other contractors under the same roof. This is a better approach to take as it makes it easier for you to budget for everything and even request a significant discount which saves you money in the long run. You’ll also avoid the potential headaches of dealing with different people. Typically, bathroom renovations cost between $9000 and $40,000. This includes the cost of labor and materials needed to remodel the space.
